Executive Brief

SOVR Development Holdings, LLC is a Wyoming-based fintech company developing next-generation credit infrastructure and decentralized financial systems. The Company's core products include:

  • SOVR Unified Credit Platform: A full-stack credit origination and management system with real-time underwriting capabilities
  • SOVR Hybrid Engine V2: High-performance transaction processing engine supporting 10,000+ TPS
  • SOVR Oracle Ledger: Blockchain-based distributed ledger for immutable audit trails
  • SOVR Credit Bridge POS: Point-of-sale financing solution with instant credit decisioning

The Company is seeking a $12 million senior secured credit facility to accelerate product development, expand market reach, and strengthen its balance sheet for scale. The loan will be secured by a pledge of 400,000 membership interests (approximately 28.6% of issued capital post-pledge), with a $2.4 million stand-by letter of credit providing additional collateral support.
