ARTICLE VII
TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTERESTS

7.1 General Restriction on Transfer

No Member shall Transfer all or any portion of such Member's Membership Interest without the prior written consent of Members holding at least seventy-five percent (75%) of the Percentage Interests (excluding the Percentage Interest of the transferring Member), except as expressly permitted by this Article VII.

7.2 Permitted Transfers

Notwithstanding Section 7.1, a Member may Transfer all or any portion of such Member's Membership Interest without Member consent in the following circumstances:

7.2.1 Transfers to Family Members. A Member who is an individual may Transfer Membership Interests to such Member's spouse, children, grandchildren, parents, siblings, or trusts for the benefit of such persons.

7.2.2 Transfers to Affiliates. A Member that is an entity may Transfer Membership Interests to any entity that controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with such Member.

7.2.3 Transfers by Operation of Law. Transfers by will, intestate succession, or operation of law upon the death or incapacity of a Member.

7.2.4 Conditions. Any Transfer pursuant to this Section 7.2 shall be subject to the following conditions: (a) the transferee must execute a joinder to this Agreement agreeing to be bound by all of its terms; (b) the transferring Member must provide written notice to the Company at least thirty (30) days prior to the Transfer; and (c) the transferring Member shall remain liable for any obligations to the Company that arose prior to the Transfer.

7.3 Procedure for Valid Transfer

For any Transfer to be valid and effective:

  • (a) The transferring Member must provide written notice to the Company and all other Members at least sixty (60) days prior to the proposed Transfer, including the identity of the proposed transferee, the number of Membership Interests to be transferred, and the terms and conditions of the Transfer;
  • (b) The transferee must execute a joinder to this Agreement agreeing to be bound by all of its terms and conditions;
  • (c) The Transfer must comply with all applicable federal and state securities laws;
  • (d) The transferring Member must pay all costs and expenses associated with the Transfer, including reasonable attorneys' fees incurred by the Company;
  • (e) The Transfer must not violate any provision of this Agreement or cause the Company to be dissolved or terminated;
  • (f) If required by this Agreement, the Transfer must be approved by the requisite percentage of Members.

7.4 Rights of Transferee

7.4.1 Economic Rights Only. Unless otherwise agreed by Members holding at least seventy-five percent (75%) of the Percentage Interests, a transferee of a Membership Interest (other than a Permitted Transfer under Section 7.2) shall be entitled only to the economic rights associated with the transferred Membership Interest, including the right to receive distributions and allocations of income, gain, loss, and deduction. The transferee shall not be entitled to vote, participate in management, receive information, or exercise any other rights of a Member without Member approval.

7.4.2 Admission as Member. A transferee may be admitted as a substitute Member only upon the written consent of Members holding at least seventy-five percent (75%) of the Percentage Interests and the execution of a joinder to this Agreement.

7.5 Consequences of Invalid Transfer

Any attempted Transfer in violation of this Article VII shall be null and void and of no force or effect. The Company shall not recognize any such Transfer and shall not reflect the Transfer in the Company's books and records.

7.6 Certificates of Ownership

The Company may, but shall not be required to, issue certificates evidencing Membership Interests. Any such certificates shall be in such form as determined by the Manager and shall contain a legend restricting transfer in accordance with this Agreement.

ARTICLE VIII
RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL, DRAG-ALONG & TAG-ALONG

8.1 Right of First Refusal

8.1.1 Applicability. If any Member (the "Offering Member") receives a bona fide written offer from a third party (the "Offeror") to purchase all or any portion of such Member's Membership Interest, and the Offering Member desires to accept such offer, the Offering Member must first offer to sell such Membership Interest to the other Members (the "Non-Offering Members") in accordance with this Section 8.1.

8.1.2 Offer Notice. The Offering Member shall provide written notice (the "Offer Notice") to the Company and all Non-Offering Members. The Offer Notice shall include: (a) the identity of the Offeror; (b) the number of Membership Interests proposed to be sold; (c) the purchase price and other material terms and conditions of the offer; and (d) a copy of the written offer from the Offeror.

8.1.3 Exercise Period. Each Non-Offering Member shall have thirty (30) days from receipt of the Offer Notice to elect to purchase all or a portion of the offered Membership Interests at the same price and on the same terms and conditions as set forth in the Offer Notice. If more than one Non-Offering Member elects to purchase, the offered Membership Interests shall be allocated among them in proportion to their respective Percentage Interests.

8.1.4 Closing. If the Non-Offering Members elect to purchase all of the offered Membership Interests, the closing shall occur within sixty (60) days after the expiration of the exercise period. If the Non-Offering Members do not elect to purchase all of the offered Membership Interests, the Offering Member may sell the offered Membership Interests to the Offeror within ninety (90) days after the expiration of the exercise period, at a price and on terms no more favorable to the Offeror than those set forth in the Offer Notice.

8.2 Drag-Along Right

8.2.1 Exercise of Drag-Along Right. If Members holding at least seventy-five percent (75%) of the Percentage Interests (the "Drag-Along Members") receive a bona fide offer from a third party to purchase all of the outstanding Membership Interests of the Company, and the Drag-Along Members desire to accept such offer, the Drag-Along Members may require all other Members (the "Dragged Members") to sell their Membership Interests to the third party on the same terms and conditions.

8.2.2 Drag-Along Notice. The Drag-Along Members shall provide written notice (the "Drag-Along Notice") to all Dragged Members at least thirty (30) days prior to the proposed closing. The Drag-Along Notice shall include: (a) the identity of the purchaser; (b) the purchase price and other material terms and conditions of the sale; and (c) the proposed closing date.

8.2.3 Obligations of Dragged Members. Upon receipt of a Drag-Along Notice, each Dragged Member shall be obligated to: (a) sell all of such Member's Membership Interests to the purchaser at the same price per unit and on the same terms and conditions as the Drag-Along Members; (b) execute all documents and instruments reasonably necessary to consummate the sale; (c) make customary representations and warranties; and (d) take all actions reasonably necessary to facilitate the closing.

8.2.4 Pro Rata Allocation. The purchase price shall be allocated among all Members (including both Drag-Along Members and Dragged Members) in proportion to their respective Percentage Interests, unless otherwise agreed by all Members.

8.3 Tag-Along Right

8.3.1 Exercise of Tag-Along Right. If any Member (the "Selling Member") proposes to Transfer Membership Interests to a third party (other than a Permitted Transfer under Section 7.2), each other Member (the "Tag-Along Member") shall have the right to participate in such Transfer on the same terms and conditions.

8.3.2 Tag-Along Notice. The Selling Member shall provide written notice (the "Tag-Along Notice") to all Tag-Along Members at least thirty (30) days prior to the proposed Transfer. The Tag-Along Notice shall include: (a) the identity of the purchaser; (b) the number of Membership Interests proposed to be sold; (c) the purchase price per unit and other material terms and conditions; and (d) the proposed closing date.

8.3.3 Exercise Period. Each Tag-Along Member shall have twenty (20) days from receipt of the Tag-Along Notice to elect to participate in the Transfer by providing written notice to the Selling Member.

8.3.4 Allocation. If one or more Tag-Along Members elect to participate in the Transfer, the total number of Membership Interests to be sold to the purchaser shall be allocated among the Selling Member and the participating Tag-Along Members in proportion to their respective Percentage Interests. The Selling Member shall reduce the number of Membership Interests it sells to accommodate the Tag-Along Members.

8.3.5 Closing. The closing of the Transfer shall occur within sixty (60) days after the expiration of the exercise period. All participating Members shall execute all documents and instruments reasonably necessary to consummate the Transfer and shall make customary representations and warranties.

ARTICLE IX
BUY-SELL / DEAD-LOCK RESOLUTION

9.1 Trigger Events

The buy-sell provisions of this Article IX may be invoked upon the occurrence of any of the following events:

  • (a) Voluntary Withdrawal. A Member provides notice of intent to withdraw from the Company;
  • (b) Bankruptcy or Insolvency. A Member becomes subject to bankruptcy, insolvency, or similar proceedings;
  • (c) Death or Incapacity. The death or incapacity of a Member who is an individual;
  • (d) Dissolution of Entity Member. The dissolution or termination of a Member that is an entity;
  • (e) Material Breach. A Member materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice;
  • (f) Deadlock. The Members are unable to agree on a material decision requiring Member approval for a period of ninety (90) days or more.

9.2 Buy-Sell Procedure

9.2.1 Offer to Sell. Upon the occurrence of a trigger event, any Member (the "Initiating Member") may deliver written notice (the "Buy-Sell Notice") to all other Members offering to sell all (but not less than all) of the Initiating Member's Membership Interests at a specified price per unit.

9.2.2 Response Period. Within thirty (30) days after receipt of the Buy-Sell Notice, each other Member (the "Responding Member") must elect to either: (a) purchase all of the Initiating Member's Membership Interests at the specified price; or (b) sell all of such Responding Member's Membership Interests to the Initiating Member at the same price per unit.

9.2.3 Multiple Responding Members. If more than one Responding Member elects to purchase the Initiating Member's Membership Interests, they shall purchase such interests in proportion to their respective Percentage Interests. If any Responding Member elects to sell, the Initiating Member must purchase the Membership Interests of all Members who elected to sell.
