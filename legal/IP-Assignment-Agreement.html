RECITALS

A. The Assignor has been engaged by the Assignee (the "Engagement") to perform services related to the development of proprietary software, technology, and intellectual property (collectively, the "Services").

B. In the course of performing the Services, the Assignor has conceived, created, developed, or otherwise contributed to certain inventions, software code, designs, documentation, and other intellectual property (collectively, the "IP").

C. The parties desire to evidence that all right, title, and interest in and to the IP is the sole and exclusive property of the Assignee.

AGREEMENT

1. Assignment of IP

The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Assignee, its successors and assigns, all right, title, and interest in and to the IP, including all worldwide copyrights, patent rights, trade secret rights, and all other intellectual property rights therein. The IP includes, without limitation, all source code, object code, documentation, designs, specifications, algorithms, processes, methodologies, and any derivative works or improvements thereof.

2. Representations & Warranties

The Assignor represents and warrants that:

  • a. The Assignor has the full right and authority to enter into this Agreement;
  • b. The IP is original to the Assignor and does not infringe any third-party rights;
  • c. The Assignor has not previously assigned, transferred, or encumbered any rights to the IP;
  • d. The Assignor will execute any further documents reasonably necessary to perfect the Assignee's ownership of the IP.

3. Confidentiality

The Assignor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information of the Assignee and to comply with the Assignee's Non-Disclosure Agreement, dated [Date] (the "NDA"), which is incorporated by reference herein.

4. Work Made for Hire

The parties acknowledge and agree that all IP created by the Assignor in the course of the Services constitutes "work made for hire" for the Assignee under applicable copyright law. To the extent any IP does not qualify as work made for hire, the assignment under Section 1 above shall apply.

5. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Wyoming, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions. Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in [County], Wyoming, and the parties consent to the jurisdiction of such courts.

6. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with the NDA, constitutes the entire understanding between the parties concerning the subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements.

7. Counterparts & Electronic Signatures

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be an original, and all of which together shall constitute one instrument. Electronic signatures (including via DocuSign or similar) shall have the same force and effect as handwritten signatures.
