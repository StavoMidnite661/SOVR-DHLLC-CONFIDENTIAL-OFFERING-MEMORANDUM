COVENANT COMPLIANCE NOTES

The Company will maintain the following financial covenants throughout the term of the credit facility:

  • Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR): Minimum 1.25x, calculated quarterly
  • Leverage Ratio: Maximum 3.0x Debt to EBITDA
  • Liquidity: Minimum $500,000 unrestricted cash balance at all times
  • Collateral Value: Pledged membership interests must maintain minimum fair market value of $15,000,000 (subject to annual appraisal)

The Use of Proceeds Schedule is incorporated by reference into the Credit Agreement and may be amended with Lender consent.
