4. GRANT OF SECURITY INTEREST

4.1 Pledge: Pledgor hereby pledges, assigns, and grants to the Secured Party a first-priority security interest in the Collateral to secure the prompt and complete payment and performance of the Secured Obligations.

4.2 Perfection: Pledgor shall execute and deliver all such instruments and documents (including UCC-1 financing statements) and take all such actions as the Secured Party may reasonably request to perfect and maintain the first-priority security interest.

4.3 Notice of Perfection: Pledgor shall provide the Secured Party with written evidence of the filing of the UCC-1 financing statement within five (5) business days of the Effective Date.

5. REPRESENTATIONS & WARRANTIES

  1. Ownership: Pledgor is the legal and beneficial owner of the Pledged Interests, free and clear of any lien, security interest, or encumbrance, except for the security interest created by this Agreement.
  2. Authority: Pledgor has the full power, authority, and legal right to execute and deliver this Agreement and to pledge the Collateral.
  3. Enforceability: This Agreement constitutes a legal, valid, and binding obligation of the Pledgor, enforceable in accordance with its terms.
  4. No Conflict: The execution and delivery of this Agreement will not violate any law, regulation, or contractual obligation of the Pledgor.
  5. Perfected Interest: Upon the filing of the UCC-1 financing statement, the Secured Party will have a valid and perfected first-priority security interest in the Collateral.

6. COVENANTS

6.1 Prohibited Actions

  • a. No Additional Liens: Pledgor shall not create, incur, or permit to exist any lien or encumbrance on the Collateral other than the security interest created by this Agreement.
  • b. No Transfer: Pledgor shall not sell, assign, transfer, or otherwise dispose of any part of the Collateral without the prior written consent of the Secured Party.
  • c. No Material Change: Pledgor shall not permit any material change to the organizational documents of the Company that would adversely affect the Secured Party's interest.

6.2 Notification & Reporting

  1. Pledgor shall promptly notify the Secured Party of any lien or claim made or asserted against the Collateral.
  2. Pledgor shall provide quarterly compliance certificates confirming the status of the Collateral.
  3. Pledgor shall provide annual audited financial statements of the Company.
  4. Pledgor shall notify the Secured Party of any Event of Default or potential Event of Default within two (2) business days.

7. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

  1. Failure by the Pledgor to pay any principal or interest on the Secured Obligations when due.
  2. Any representation or warranty made by the Pledgor herein or in any Loan Document proves to have been incorrect in any material respect when made.
  3. Breach of any covenant or agreement contained in this Agreement or the Loan Documents.
  4. The insolvency, bankruptcy, or dissolution of the Pledgor or the Company.
  5. A material adverse change in the business, operations, or financial condition of the Pledgor or the Company.

8. REMEDIES

  1. The Secured Party may exercise all rights and remedies of a secured party under the Uniform Commercial Code (UCC).
  2. The Secured Party may sell, assign, or otherwise dispose of the Collateral at public or private sale.
  3. The Secured Party may transfer the Pledged Interests into its own name or the name of its nominee.
  4. The Secured Party may collect any distributions or proceeds from the Collateral and apply them to the Secured Obligations.
  5. The Secured Party may pursue any other remedy available at law or in equity.

Cumulative Remedies: The rights and remedies provided in this Agreement are cumulative and not exclusive of any rights or remedies provided by law or the Loan Documents.

9. GOVERNING LAW & JURISDICTION

This Agreement and the rights and obligations of the parties hereunder shall be governed by, and construed and interpreted in accordance with, the laws of the State of Wyoming, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. Any legal action or proceeding with respect to this Agreement shall be brought in the courts of the State of Wyoming or of the United States of America for the District of Wyoming.
